MY Hobbies

  • Reading: Whether it's fiction, non-fiction, or a specific genre like mystery, science fiction, or fantasy, reading can be a rewarding hobby.
  • Writing: Express yourself through writing. You can start a journal, write short stories, or even try your hand at poetry.
  • Cooking/Baking: Experiment with different recipes and cuisines. Cooking can be a creative and delicious hobby.
  • Gardening: Cultivate plants, flowers, or even a vegetable garden. Gardening can be therapeutic and rewarding.
  • Drawing/Painting: Explore your artistic side through drawing, painting, sketching, or other forms of visual art.
